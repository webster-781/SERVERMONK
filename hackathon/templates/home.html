About Us

Who are we?

Foodtrack is an web-app which enables you to track the your daily calories input , protein input and carbohydrate input .

You can set daily goals for your diet an keep track of what you eat. Also you can your generate monthly diet habbits

Why Foodtracker ?

Your life, Your choice: Are you a gym freak, a guy trying to cut his fat or just a person who wants to make sure they live a healthy lifestyle? We got everything covered. choose your own diet plan and start tracking the daily nourishment you get.
